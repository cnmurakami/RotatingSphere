Note for adding Spheres:

Just drop the prefab at Resources\Prefabs\Globes and it will be added automatically;
The prefab must have RotateSphere2.cs script attached to it with proper speed;
Names shall be give in the format aa-Name, whereas aa stands for classification
Any character after 'aa-' will be shown at Globe Selection screen (case sensitive)
